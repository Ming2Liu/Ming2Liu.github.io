Research Profile
======

My research focuses on applied machine learning, optimisation, control, and trustworthy AI for intelligent, data-driven systems. I am particularly interested in developing scalable and operationally robust AI methods for smart mobility, smart cities, energy systems, cloud computing, healthcare, and environmental intelligence.

My work combines machine learning, distributed intelligence, graph-based modelling, optimisation, privacy-preserving analytics, and human-centred decision support for real-world cyber-physical and socio-technical systems.
